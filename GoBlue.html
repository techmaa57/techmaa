GoBlue The change starts with you
GoBlue is a mobile application, its main objective is to sensitize or spread awareness about the importance of water conservation to avoid needless waste and optimize water use through a set of advantages provided to users, and among these advantages:
  • Provide information on daily and monthly consumption
  • Providing a consumption chart and additional tips for less consumption
  • Rewards based on the points collection system
  • Link you with plumber specialists
  • Allowing users to conveniently track their water usage and compete with friends
Requirements:
Compatible with iPhone and iPad. Requires iPhone OS 7 or later. WiFi or 4G network connection sometimes required.
Requirements:
Requires Android 4.1.1 and higher. WiFi or 4G network connection sometimes required.
